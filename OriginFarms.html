About


Origin Farms is rice growing ground on Plum Hill Plantation in South Carolina.



Lucie Kulze founded the farm in 2015 and seeks to renew the rice plantation that was once there.
The farm's vision is to connect the community to locally grown, seasonal produce even beyond the rice crop.
Today, you can find Origin Farm's rice in the downtown Charleston's local farmers market. Look out for it!

History



Carolina Gold Rice is both a traditional and heirloom crop.
Rice cultivation was born in the United States in Charleston, SC
around 1685. On Plum Hill Plantation, the rice is grown and
harvested in historic paddy fields.

"Brown rice is by far the most nutritional rice form available. Because of the severe milling and polishing process, it is devoid of practically all vitamins and nutrients."

-Dr. M.T.Murray, ND
